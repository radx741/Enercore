Ring Size Calculator

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Cut a thin strip of paper or use string.
  2. Wrap it around the base of your finger snugly (not too tight).
  3. Mark the point where the end meets the rest of the strip.
  4. Lay it flat and measure the length in centimeters.
  5. Enter the value below to calculate your ring size.
